# SLA Policy

## Uptime SLA
We guarantee a 99.9% uptime SLA for all services.

## Incident Response Times
- **P0 (Critical)**: 1 hour
- **P1 (High)**: 4 hours
- **P2 (Medium)**: 24 hours
- **P3 (Low)**: 48 hours

## Credit Calculation Formula
If uptime falls below 99.9% in a given month, customers are eligible for service credits.
P0 incidents exceeding 4h entitle customer to 10% monthly credit.

## RCA Delivery SLA
Root Cause Analysis (RCA) reports will be delivered within 24 hours for P0 incidents.
