# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA BAA Availability
We can sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for Enterprise customers requiring HIPAA compliance.

## GDPR DPA Process
For GDPR compliance, we offer a standard Data Processing Agreement (DPA). Customers can request a DPA via support.
**Article 20 (Data Portability)**: Formal GDPR Article 20 data portability requests are time-boxed to a 30-day statutory deadline. 

## SOC 2 Type II Status
We are SOC 2 Type II compliant. The report is available upon request under NDA.

## Data Residency Options
We offer data residency in the US and EU. Enterprise customers can choose their preferred region.
