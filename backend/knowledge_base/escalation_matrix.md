# Escalation Matrix

## Who Handles What

- **Legal Threats**: Route to the Legal team.
- **Security Incidents (e.g. Ransomware)**: Route to the Security queue immediately. Never auto-reply.
- **PR Crises / Public Review Threats**: Route to the PR/Communications team and Account Manager.
- **VIP Churns**: Route to the Account Manager and Customer Success Lead.
- **GDPR Requests**: Route to the Compliance team.
