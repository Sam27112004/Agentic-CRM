# Pricing Policy

## Pricing Tiers
- **Starter**: $49/month. Includes basic features, 1 user, community support.
- **Standard**: $99/month. Includes standard features, up to 5 users, priority email support.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing. Includes advanced features, unlimited users, dedicated account manager.

## Non-profit Discounts
We offer a 30% discount off the Standard tier for verified non-profit organizations.

## Pro-rata Billing Rules
When upgrading or downgrading, billing is prorated. Upgrades take effect immediately and the prorated amount is charged. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Enterprise Custom Pricing
Enterprise custom pricing is determined on a case-by-case basis. Contact sales for details.
