# Refund Policy

## General Policy
No refunds will be issued after 14 days of the initial purchase or renewal.

## Exception Process
Exceptions to the 14-day rule can only be granted by the billing manager for extreme circumstances (e.g., prolonged service outage).

## Credits vs Refunds
Whenever possible, we prefer to issue service credits instead of cash refunds.

## Churn Retention Playbook
If a customer is threatening to churn or write a public review due to a bad experience, support agents may suggest a retention offer, such as offering a 1-month free credit or a 20% discount on their next renewal.
